Build a web app demo called "Jio TeleOS — Payments Demo". It's a two-panel interactive prototype showing how payments work on a smart TV OS.                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  Layout:                                                                                                                                                                                        
  - Full viewport, dark background                                                                                                                                                               
  - Top: a row of scenario tabs (clickable, one active at a time)                                                                                                                                
  - Center: two device mockups side by side — a widescreen TV frame on the left and a portrait phone frame on the right, connected by an animated arc that glows when a handoff is happening     
  between the two devices                                   
  - Bottom: step indicator dots and back/next buttons

  Interaction:
  - Clicking on the TV screen advances the demo step
  - The phone reacts automatically based on the current step
  - Each scenario is independent — clicking a tab resets to step 1 of that scenario

  Scenarios to include (each with 4–6 steps):
  1. 🏏 IPL Live Gate — cricket match goes PPV mid-stream, user pays ₹49 to continue watching
  2. 🎬 OTT Subscription — user hits a paywall, selects annual plan, pays via UPI QR
  3. 🎮 Gaming Top-up — in-game purchase triggers low wallet warning, user tops up JioPay via QR
  4. 👨‍👩‍👧 Family Auth — child initiates purchase, parent gets approval request on their phone, approves it
  5. 🛒 T-Commerce — shoppable ad appears mid-show, user buys product from JioMart without leaving content
  6. 🎵 JioSaavn Premium — music subscription paywall and QR checkout
  7. 📋 Subscription Hub — view all active subscriptions, cancel flow with win-back offer
  8. 📱 Mobile Recharge — recharge a family member's Jio number from the TV

  TV screen content: Each step should show a realistic TV OS UI — dark cinematic backgrounds, glassmorphism overlay cards, large bold text, payment method selection (JioPay wallet, UPI QR,
  saved card), QR code screen, waiting/spinner state, and success state.

  Phone screen content: Idle/lock screen, push notification for payment request, UPI PIN entry screen, payment success screen. For family auth: approval card with Approve/Deny buttons.

  Stack: React + Tailwind CSS + Framer Motion for transitions. No backend needed — all frontend state.

  Design: Dark theme (#060914 background), electric blue accent, cinematic feel. TV screens should look like a real TV OS (think Netflix/Prime UI), not a mobile app on a big screen. Phone
  screens should look like a real Android phone.
