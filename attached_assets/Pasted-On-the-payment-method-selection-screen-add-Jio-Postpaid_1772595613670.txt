On the payment method selection screen, add Jio Postpaid Billing as a fourth option alongside UPI / Scan QR, JioPay Wallet, and Saved Card.                  
                                                                                                                                                               
  Make all four payment methods fully functional with their own distinct flows:                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                               
  JioPay Wallet — TV shows wallet balance and a confirmation screen ("Pay ₹X from JioPay Wallet?"). One tap confirms. Instant success on TV. No phone          
  interaction, no QR, no handoff arc — the wallet is pre-authenticated on the account.                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                             
  Jio Postpaid Billing — TV shows a confirmation screen ("Add ₹X to your Jio bill?"). One tap confirms. Instant success on TV with a note that it will appear
  on the next Jio bill. No phone interaction needed, no handoff arc.

  Saved Card — TV shows the saved card (masked number, bank name). Phone receives an OTP notification. Phone panel shows an OTP entry screen. User confirms.
  Both TV and phone show success. Handoff arc animates.

  No saved card (new card flow) — if no card is saved, TV shows "Add a card from your phone" with a QR code. Scanning opens a secure card-addition page in the
  phone browser with two options: (1) Tap your card — hold physical card to back of phone, NFC reads and auto-fills card details; (2) Enter manually — standard
   card entry form. After card is added it gets tokenized and saved to the Jio account. TV confirms instantly and payment completes. Handoff arc animates
  throughout.

  The handoff arc between TV and phone should only animate for UPI, Saved Card, and New Card flows — not for JioPay Wallet or Postpaid Billing since those
  require no cross-device interaction.
